INCIDENT REPORT — REAL, VERIFIED

Incident ID: INC-REAL-2025-001
Date: June 8, 2025
Facility: Visakhapatnam Steel Plant (Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited - RINL)
Location: Steel Melt Shop-1, Caster-2
Equipment Involved: Ladle (molten steel transfer vessel), overhead crane

Description:
At approximately 4:15 PM, Caster-2 of Steel Melt Shop-1 at Visakhapatnam Steel Plant was
in the process of casting. Before the slide gate could open to pour hot crude steel from
the ladle into the tundish (and subsequently to the caster), a sudden and severe explosion
occurred. A severe fireball rose to the ceiling, and Overhead Crane-2 caught fire as a result.

Casualties: 8 workers died from injuries sustained in the explosion. 6 additional workers
were injured and hospitalized, with 2 in critical condition at the time of initial reporting.

Preliminary Cause Assessment:
Visakhapatnam Regional Fire Officer D. Niranjan Reddy stated the blast may have been
triggered by impurities in the molten metal, or may have occurred due to a process-related
issue. As of the initial Ministry of Steel statement, the exact root cause had not yet been
determined and was under investigation.

Investigation Status:
Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited (RINL) opened an internal investigation. Additionally, the
Ministry of Steel assigned a 3-member external enquiry committee, led by the Director-in-
Charge of Bokaro (SAIL), to investigate the accident independently.

Official Statement:
Union Minister of State for Heavy Industries and Steel, Bhupathiraju Srinivasa Varma,
confirmed the incident publicly and stated industrial safety remains a top priority,
extending support to affected workers and families.

Source: Ministry of Steel, Government of India (public statement); news coverage including
Zee News, June 2025.
